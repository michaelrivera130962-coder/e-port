THE PERSON
BEHIND THE PAGE

I'm Mikey Rivera — a college freshman majoring in Fashion Marketing & Product Development at Florida State University. I've been enamored with the world of fashion for the past two years, and over the past few months I've seriously started to consider it as a career path.

But passion without accountability means nothing to me. I'm outspoken about the environment, human rights, and body positivity — and I believe the fashion industry has a responsibility to do better. This portfolio represents my first deep dive into that tension: between loving fashion and demanding it be better.
